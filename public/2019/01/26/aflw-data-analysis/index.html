AFLW + fitzRoy: Analysing women's footy using match data

January 26, 2019 Jacquie Tran

1 minute read

(source: Wikimedia)

The countdown is well and truly on.

We are but 1 week away from the first bounce of AFLW 3.0, the 3rd season of the women’s national Australian football league. As an Aussie living abroad, a footy fan, and supporter of the increasing professionalisation of women’s sport, there’s plenty to look forward to as the next chapter of the AFLW story unfolds.

How will Geelong and North Melbourne, new teams joining the national league in 2019, fare against the 8 teams who have participated in the first 2 AFLW seasons?
